Naruto

Episode 1: Naruto Uzumaki!

Naruto Uzumaki, a mischievous and often lonely 12-year-old ninja, is seen causing trouble in the Hidden Leaf Village. He's notorious for his pranks, including defacing the Hokage Rock monument.After failing to graduate from the Ninja Academy, Naruto is apprehended by his teacher, Iruka Umino. Naruto is then tricked by a rogue ninja named Mizuki into stealing a forbidden scroll. When Iruka finds Naruto with the scroll, Mizuki reveals the truth about the Nine-Tailed Fox demon sealed within him, explaining the villagers' ostracization. Iruka defends Naruto, relating to his loneliness, and Naruto, to protect Iruka, uses the Shadow Clone Jutsu to defeat Mizuki. Finally, Iruka acknowledges Naruto's strength and allows him to graduate.
