Bleach

Episode 1: The Day I Became a Shinigami

Ichigo Kurosaki, a 15-year-old with the ability to see ghosts. His life takes a dramatic turn when he encounters Rukia Kuchiki, a Soul Reaper, who is injured while battling a Hollow, a monstrous spirit that devours souls. To save his family and defeat the Hollow, Ichigo reluctantly accepts Rukia's offer to temporarily take on her Soul Reaper powers. In the process, he unexpectedly absorbs almost all of her spiritual energy, transforming him into a powerful, albeit substitute, Soul Reaper. The episode concludes with Ichigo defeating the Hollow and beginning his unexpected journey into the world of Soul Reapers and the supernatural.
