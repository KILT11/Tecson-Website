Fullmetal Alchemist

Episode 2: Body of the Sanctioned

Elric brothers arrive in a desert city called Lior. Here, they encounter a charismatic priest named Father Cornello, who appears to perform miracles, attracting a large following. Ed and Al investigate, suspecting that Cornello is using alchemy, not divine power, to deceive the people. They uncover his use of a Philosopher's Stone, or what they believe to be one, to perform these "miracles." The episode explores themes of faith, manipulation, and the brothers' relentless pursuit of the Philosopher's Stone to restore their bodies, while also hinting at a larger conspiracy at play.
