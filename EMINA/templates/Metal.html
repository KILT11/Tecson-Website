Fullmetal Alchemist

Episode 1: Those Who Challenge the Sun

Elric brothers, Edward and Alphonse, as they attempt a forbidden alchemical ritual: human transmutation. Driven by their desire to resurrect their deceased mother, Trisha, the brothers tragically fail, resulting in severe consequences. Edward loses his left leg, and Alphonse loses his entire body, his soul bound to a suit of armor by Edward's sacrifice of his right arm. This devastating experience sets the stage for their journey to uncover the secrets of the Philosopher's Stone, hoping it will restore their bodies and undo the damage caused by their alchemical transgression.
