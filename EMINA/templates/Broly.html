Broly, making him a canonical character in the Dragon Ball universe. The film delves into the history of the Saiyans, revealing that King Vegeta, fearing Broly's immense power, exiles him to Planet Vampa. Years later, Frieza learns of Broly and his father, Paragus, and recruits them to defeat Goku and Vegeta. Cheelai and Lemo, members of Frieza's army, befriend Broly and steal the remote that Paragus uses to control him. Goku and Vegeta encounter Broly, and a fierce battle ensues. Broly's power and adaptability prove to be a significant challenge, even for Goku and Vegeta in their Super Saiyan God and Super Saiyan Blue forms.
