Demon Slayer

Episode 1: Cruelty

Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted young man who lives with his family in the mountains and sells charcoal for a living. One day, Tanjiro returns home to find his family brutally murdered, with only his sister Nezuko surviving, but having been turned into a demon. Tanjiro is shocked and desperate to save her. During a confrontation with Giyu Tomioka, a demon slayer, Tanjiro shows his determination and potential, leading Giyu to direct him to a former demon slayer for training. The episode ends with Tanjiro beginning his journey to find a way to turn Nezuko back into a human and avenge his family.
